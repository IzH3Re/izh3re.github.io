About

A pathway programme for students to pursue studies in Japan and produce skilled engineers to increase national economy. 3 years in Diploma in Mechanical Engineering & Diploma in Electrical Engineering taught fully in Japanese in UniKL MFI. Graduates will pursue Bachelors in Japan for 2 years. Click here to know more about Japanese Universities Programme.

Egg Drops Contest

Test your knowledge to land an egg fast and safely to their destination without getting hurt on its way.

運動会 (Sports Day)

An annual event hosted for all JUP students to establish friendship and experience different sports from Japan.

紙車 (Papercar Contest)

A Semester 1 Contest to encourage Japanese students to know more about engineering by working in groups for whoever the fastest car, wins.
